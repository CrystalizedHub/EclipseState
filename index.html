📜 Server Rulebook

Every player must follow these rules. 6 warnings = automatic permanent ban.

🏛 General🎭 Roleplay⚔ VDM/RDM🚫 Metagaming🔫 Combat🚗 Vehicles💰 Economy🛡 Staff💬 Discord

Critical General Conduct

  • Respect everyone. Racism, homophobia, hate speech = zero tolerance, instant permanent ban.
  • No cheating. Hacking, modding, or exploiting = permanent ban, no appeal.
  • No impersonation of staff, admins, or players.
  • English only for IC and OOC communication.
  • Staff have final say. Dispute via ticket, not in-game.
  • Record sessions. Evidence protects you. Fake evidence = ban.
⚠ Warning → 3-Day Ban → Permanent Ban

Critical Roleplay Standards

  • Stay in character. Fail RP = punishment.
  • Value your life. Guns pointed at you = comply. No suicide/combat log.
  • New Life Rule: Upon death, forget everything. Stay away 15 minutes.
  • Fear RP: Act afraid in danger.
  • No powergaming. Let others react before forcing actions.
  • Realistic characters. No super-soldiers without staff approval.
  • Robberies need RP reasoning. No chain-robbing.
⚠ Warning → 7-Day Ban → Permanent Ban

Major VDM & RDM

  • RDM: Attacking/killing without valid RP = ban.
  • VDM: Running people over/vehicle as weapon = ban.
  • Every hostile action needs clear quality RP beforehand.
  • Target must understand they're being engaged before shots fire.
  • Drive-bys require prior escalation.
⚠ 3-Day → 14-Day → Permanent Ban

Major Metagaming

  • No metagaming: Using OOC info (Discord, streams) IC = ban.
  • Stream-sniping = permanent ban.
  • No external comms for IC coordination.
  • Don't look up players OOC for IC advantage.
  • Keep /ooc out of active RP.
⚠ 7-Day → Permanent Ban

Major Combat

  • Combat must stem from genuine escalated RP.
  • Max 6 per side in hostiles.
  • Capped 3 robberies/hour per player.
  • No animation/emote exploits.
  • No camping hospitals, PD, spawns.
  • 30-min cooldown before re-engaging.
⚠ 3-Day → 14-Day → Permanent Ban

Minor Vehicles

  • Drive realistically — no 120mph through city.
  • Off-roading supercars = Fail RP.
  • No scaling buildings with vehicles.
  • Aircraft require IC pilot license.
  • Stolen vehicles go through proper IC channels.
⚠ Warning → 3-Day → 7-Day Ban

Minor Economy

  • No large transfers without staff approval.
  • Duping/exploiting = perm ban + asset wipe.
  • Businesses must be actively RP'd.
  • One primary residence per character.
⚠ Warning → Asset Wipe → Permanent Ban

Major Staff & Appeals

  • Staff decisions are final during situations.
  • Never ask for free items/money.
  • False reports = you get punished.
  • Harassing staff = severe ban.
  • Appeals via Discord ticket with evidence.
⚠ 3-Day → Permanent Ban

Minor Discord

  • Follow Discord ToS.
  • No spamming/ghost pinging/flooding.
  • Right channels: memes→#media, support→tickets.
  • No ear-rape/soundboards in VC.
  • Ban evading = permanent community ban.
⚠ Mute → Kick → Ban
