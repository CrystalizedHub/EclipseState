Server Rulebook

Every player must follow these rules. 6 warnings = automatic permanent ban.

GeneralRoleplayVDM/RDMMetagamingCombatVehiclesEconomyStaffDiscord

Critical General Conduct

  • Respect everyone. Racism, homophobia, hate speech = zero tolerance, instant permanent ban.
  • No cheating. Hacking, modding, exploiting = permanent ban, no appeal.
  • No impersonation of staff, admins, or other players.
  • English only for IC and OOC communication.
  • Staff have final say. Dispute via ticket, not in-game argument.
  • Record sessions. Evidence protects you. Fake evidence = ban.
Warning → 3-Day Ban → Permanent Ban

Critical Roleplay Standards

  • Stay in character. Fail RP = punishment.
  • Value your life. Guns on you = comply. No suicide/combat log to escape.
  • New Life Rule: Upon death, forget everything. Stay away 15 minutes.
  • Fear RP: Act afraid in dangerous situations.
  • No powergaming. Let others react before forcing actions on them.
  • Realistic characters. No super-soldiers without staff approval.
  • Robberies need RP reasoning. No chain-robbing or camping spots.
Warning → 7-Day Ban → Permanent Ban

Major VDM & RDM

  • RDM: Attacking/killing without valid RP reasoning = ban.
  • VDM: Running people over or using vehicles as weapons = ban.
  • Every hostile action requires clear, quality RP beforehand.
  • The target must understand they're being engaged before shots are fired.
  • Drive-bys require prior escalation — no random sprays.
3-Day Ban → 14-Day Ban → Permanent Ban

Major Metagaming

  • No metagaming. Using OOC info (Discord, streams, player lists) IC = ban.
  • Stream-sniping = permanent ban. No warning, no appeal.
  • No external communication to coordinate IC actions.
  • Don't look up players OOC to gain IC advantage.
  • Keep /ooc chat out of active RP scenes.
7-Day Ban → Permanent Ban

Major Combat

  • Combat must stem from genuine escalated RP — never boredom.
  • Max 6 per side in any hostile situation.
  • Capped at 3 robberies/hour per player.
  • No animation or emote exploits for combat advantage.
  • No camping hospitals, PD, spawns, or garage exits.
  • 30-minute cooldown before re-engaging the same group.
3-Day Ban → 14-Day Ban → Permanent Ban

Minor Vehicles

  • Drive realistically — no 120mph through city centers.
  • Off-roading supercars or low sports cars = Fail RP.
  • No using vehicles to scale buildings or reach glitched areas.
  • Aircraft require an IC pilot license.
  • Stolen vehicles must be roleplayed through proper IC channels.
Warning → 3-Day Ban → 7-Day Ban

Minor Economy

  • No large money transfers without staff approval.
  • Duping or exploiting economy = permanent ban + asset wipe.
  • Businesses must be actively roleplayed — inactive = revoked.
  • One primary residence per character. No hoarding.
Warning → Asset Wipe → Permanent Ban

Major Staff & Appeals

  • Staff decisions are final during active situations.
  • Never ask staff for free items, money, or special treatment.
  • False reports = you get punished instead.
  • Harassing or targeting staff in or out of character = severe ban.
  • Appeals go through Discord tickets with evidence.
3-Day Ban → Permanent Ban

Minor Discord

  • Follow Discord's Terms of Service at all times.
  • No spamming, ghost pinging, or flooding any channel.
  • Use the right channels: memes → #media, support → tickets.
  • No ear-rape, soundboards, or disruptive audio in voice channels.
  • Ban evading on Discord = permanent community-wide ban.
Mute → Kick → Ban
